# NC Car-Buyer-Helper
Financial analysis tool to help ease the trepidation of new-car buyers and break down the cascading grey area involved with buying new-to-you vehicles from the dealership with all fees broken down

Inputs: Dealership Price, MSRP, HUT Tax Exemption, Lease Term, Credit Score, NC-Specific Title Fees, Trade-in, Down Payment, Rebate and Rebate Amount, Annual and Monthly Interest Rate, Admin Fee
Outputs: MSRP % Over/ Under, Credit Score Range, Suggested Down Payment, Likely APR Range, End-of-Term Total Amount Paid, New Loan Amount, Monthly Car Payment, Net Dealership Price With/ Without Trade-In
